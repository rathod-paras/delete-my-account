What happens next?

Your request will be sent to app.parasrathod@gmail.com.

  • 1. Request review We will review your game name, email address, and optional user ID.
  • 2. Account data deletion Eligible account data linked to the selected game will be deleted where technically possible.
  • 3. Confirmation if needed If more details are required, we may contact you by email.
Some limited records may be retained if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, payment, tax, or compliance reasons.
