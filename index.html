What happens after you request deletion

Data category What we delete or de-link Notes
Account and login data Firebase Authentication account records linked to your verified app account, where applicable. We may ask for verification before deletion to prevent unauthorized account removal.
Game profile and progress Cloud save data, player profile data, gameplay progress, virtual item records, and database records linked to your account, where applicable. Local data stored only on your device may need to be removed by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage.
Support request data Support messages directly linked to your deletion request may be removed or minimized after processing, unless needed for compliance records. We retain enough information to document that the request was handled.
Analytics, ads, crash, and SDK data Where data is linked to your account and under our control, we delete, anonymize, or de-link it when technically possible. Aggregated, anonymized, or third-party-controlled SDK data may be retained by service providers according to their own policies.
Security and legal records We may retain limited records if required for fraud prevention, security, accounting, legal obligations, dispute handling, or platform compliance. Retention is limited to what is reasonably necessary for those purposes.

SDKs and services that may be involved

Our apps and games may use common game services and SDKs such as Google AdMob, Firebase Authentication, Firebase Realtime Database or Firestore, Firebase Cloud Messaging, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, AppLovin MAX, GameAnalytics, Unity Ads, LevelPlay, Meta/Facebook SDK, and similar platform or advertising tools.

These services may process identifiers, device information, analytics events, crash diagnostics, advertising data, and app interaction data as described in our Privacy Policy and the policies of those providers. Your Play Console Data safety disclosures should match the exact SDKs and data use for each individual app.
